I think about the internet the way I think about everything else - as a network of nodes and edges. Routers, CDN servers, chess pieces, people, movie plots - they're all graphs. Before this, I skated in high school, built some ML projects in undergrad, and randomly modeled my entire college in 3D because I wanted to understand Blender.

I read a lot, watch a lot of movies, and wonder about infrastructure most people don't think about - like how submarine cables route data across oceans, or how millions of video streams reach devices without everything collapsing.
